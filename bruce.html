How BRUCE Works

A deep dive into the autonomous security engine

24/7 Autonomous Monitoring

BRUCE continuously scans your entire attack surface — endpoints, networks, cloud infrastructure, and applications. No shifts, no gaps, no downtime. Every data point is ingested and analyzed in real time.

Intelligent Alert Triage

Context-aware analysis that correlates signals across your SIEM, EDR, and threat intelligence feeds. BRUCE understands the difference between a false positive and a genuine threat — and prioritizes accordingly.

Automated Incident Response

When BRUCE identifies a threat, it doesn't wait for human approval. Orchestrated response playbooks contain and remediate incidents in seconds — isolating hosts, blocking IPs, and rolling back changes automatically.

Continuous Learning

BRUCE adapts to your unique environment. It learns your network topology, user behavior patterns, and organizational workflows — getting smarter and more accurate over time with every incident it processes.

SIEM & SOAR Integration

BRUCE plugs into your existing stack — Splunk, Sentinel, Elastic, CrowdStrike, and more. It enriches your SIEM data with context and orchestrates responses through your SOAR platform, no rip-and-replace required.

Real-Time Behavioral Analysis

Using ML-powered behavioral baselines, BRUCE detects anomalous activity that signature-based tools miss — lateral movement, credential abuse, data exfiltration, and zero-day exploitation patterns.
