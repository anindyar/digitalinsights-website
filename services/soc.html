SOC Capabilities

Our Security Operations Center delivers comprehensive threat visibility and response across your entire digital estate — endpoints, networks, cloud workloads, and applications.

24/7 Continuous Monitoring

Round-the-clock surveillance of your entire attack surface — network traffic, endpoint activity, cloud workloads, identity systems, and email security. Our analysts and AI agents work in tandem to detect anomalies and suspicious behavior before they escalate into breaches.

SIEM & Log Management

Centralized security information and event management with advanced correlation rules, custom detection logic, and intelligent alerting. We ingest, normalize, and analyze logs from across your infrastructure to surface high-fidelity alerts and eliminate blind spots.

Threat Hunting

Proactive, hypothesis-driven threat hunting that goes beyond automated detection. Our experienced analysts search for advanced persistent threats (APTs), novel attack techniques, and hidden adversaries that may have evaded traditional security controls.

Incident Response

Structured incident response with automated playbooks and expert human oversight. From initial triage through containment, eradication, and recovery — we handle incidents with speed and precision, minimizing business impact and keeping stakeholders informed at every stage.

Threat Intelligence

Integration of global and regional threat intelligence feeds, enriched with UAE-specific threat data. We correlate indicators of compromise (IOCs), tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) against your environment to detect known and emerging threats targeting Middle Eastern organizations.

Technology & Integration

We deploy and manage best-of-breed security technologies, integrated into a cohesive detection and response pipeline:

EDR & XDR

Endpoint and extended detection & response platforms providing deep visibility into system-level activity, process behavior, and file operations across endpoints, servers, and cloud instances.

SOAR Automation

Security orchestration, automation, and response playbooks that accelerate containment and remediation. Automated enrichment, ticketing, and response actions reduce mean time to respond from hours to minutes.

Cloud Security Monitoring

Native integration with AWS CloudTrail, Azure Monitor, and Google Cloud logging. We monitor cloud configurations, API calls, data access patterns, and infrastructure changes to detect cloud-specific threats and misconfigurations.
